An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 1,800 sales transactions across 2023–2025 to uncover revenue trends, product performance, and customer payment behavior.

## Business Questions Answered
- Which season drives the highest revenue?
- How have yearly sales trended from 2023 to 2025?
- Which products sell the most units?
- What payment methods do customers prefer?
- How do customer ratings vary across products?

## Key Insights
- Spring generated the highest seasonal revenue at ₹9,70,600
- 2024 was the peak sales year at ₹13,23,612
- Electronics outperformed Furniture significantly in total revenue
- Tablet had the highest units sold (1,316), Printer the lowest (1,113)
- All payment methods were used almost equally (~20–22% each)

## Dashboard Features
- 3 KPI cards: Total Revenue, Total Orders, Avg Order Value
- Interactive slicers: Year, Product, Payment Method, Month
- 6 chart types: Bar, Line, Donut, Treemap, Category Bar, Rating Chart
- Fully dynamic — all charts update on slicer selection

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Slicers, Charts)
- Dataset: Public sales dataset (1,800 rows)

## Skills Demonstrated
- Data modeling with multiple related sheets
- Pivot table design and summarization
- Dashboard layout and UX thinking
- Business insight extraction from raw data
